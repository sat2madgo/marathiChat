Marathi Chatty(MC) is a great way to meet new friends.

When you use MC, we pick someone else at random and let you talk one-on-one. To help you stay safe, chats are anonymous unless you tell someone who you are (not suggested!), and you can stop a chat at any time. Predators have been known to use MC, so please be careful from those BC's.
